Sushi

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 ½ cups water
  • ¼ cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar and ½ tsp salt
  • 4 nori sheets
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • 1 cucumber (cut into thin strips)
  • 200g imitation crab sticks
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Rice: Rinse rice, cook with water, mix in heated vinegar, sugar, and salt. Let cool.
  2. Set Up: Cover bamboo mat with plastic wrap. Place nori (shiny side down), spread rice, then flip.
  3. Add Fillings: Line avocado, cucumber, and crab in the center.
  4. Roll: Roll tightly using the mat, shaping as you go.
  5. Finish: Sprinkle sesame seeds, slice into 6–8 pieces with a wet knife.
  6. Serve: Pair with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
