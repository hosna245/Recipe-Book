Sushi

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 2 ½ cups water
  • ¼ cup rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sugar and ½ tsp salt
  • 4 nori sheets
  • 1 avocado (sliced)
  • 1 cucumber (cut into thin strips)
  • 200g imitation crab sticks
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare Rice: Rinse rice, cook with water, mix in heated vinegar, sugar, and salt. Let cool.
  2. Set Up: Cover bamboo mat with plastic wrap. Place nori (shiny side down), spread rice, then flip.
  3. Add Fillings: Line avocado, cucumber, and crab in the center.
  4. Roll: Roll tightly using the mat, shaping as you go.
  5. Finish: Sprinkle sesame seeds, slice into 6–8 pieces with a wet knife.
  6. Serve: Pair with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Dessert

Ingredients:

  • 200g Dark Chocolate
  • 100g Butter
  • 150g Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 50g Flour
  • 1 tsp Vanilla Extract

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter together.
  3. Mix in sugar, eggs, and vanilla extract.
  4. Add the flour and mix until smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into greased ramekins.
  6. Bake for 12 minutes until the edges are set but the center is soft.
  7. Let it cool for 5 minutes, then serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
